About
Oneiros

Manipal University Jaipur organizes Jaipur’s biggest cultural fest, Oneiros. People eagerly wait round the year for this three day fest. Oneiros in simple words means personification of dream. The fest living upto it’s actual meaning, conducts such events wherein people get together and showcase their talents in various fields like music, dance, acting, writing, fashion, photography, and much more. The fest is not just limited to this, it also conducts entertainment activities like famous artists’ performances.

Theme

Reverie

To see dreams with open eyes is what makes them come true. Success in reality is achieved only through your dreams. Daydreaming will make you experience amazing things. This time, in Oneiros, we are going to convert your dreams into reality. And that is why the theme for this year is Reverie.
