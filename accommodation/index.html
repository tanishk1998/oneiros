Accommodation Guide

  • Rooms for staying overnight for outhouse people will be provided in our university guest house.
  • For which tariff of rooms will be Rs. 920/- (per room for 24 hours from the time of check in).
  • Maximum 2 people can be accommodated in a single room.
  • This tariff does not include meals.
  • Rooms can be pre-book by making online payment in the given online portal.
  • Meals prices will be as per our mess charge.

Terms & Conditions

Terms & Conditions

  • A government ID should be with the participants living in our guest house, which will be deposited while checking in. (Which will be returned at the time of checkout)
  • Security deposit amount Rs. 500/- cash (per person) will be collected at the time of check in. (Which will refundable at the time of check out if no damage of room found).

Rules to Follow

Rules to Follow

  • Smoking and Drinking are not allowed in the hostel premises.
  • All damages made to the room or its furniture will be chargeable to the participants.
  • No entry is allowed inside the hostel premises after 9pm.
  • All participants need to be inside their rooms after 11pm.
  • One water bottle will be provided initially, afterwards the participants need to fill up on their own from the water coolers provided in each floor.

Rules to Follow

Rules to Follow

  • Boys and Girls are to live in separate blocks.
  • Hot water is available from 6:30 to 8:00 am and 6:30 to 8:00pm only.
  • If the participants want to order food after 11:00 pm, they can order from night canteen outlets available in hostel premises.
  • Accomocation shall only be allowed to those people who are going to participate in Oneiros '18's events.
  • No mess plates are allowed to be taken to the participant rooms.

    Breakfast: - 8:00 am to 10:00 am

    Hi Tea: - 5:00 pm to 6:00 pm
