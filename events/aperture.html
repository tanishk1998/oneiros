FOCUS
Photography Exhibition

Aperture, The Official Photography Club of MUJ brings back to you the exclusive, three day long annual photography exhibition, FOCUS. The exhibition will feature some of the best photographs, films and designs produced by the students of Manipal University, Jaipur.
Artworks will be called as entries in an event on Aperture's official Facebook page www.facebook.com/aperturemuj.
This can be a platform to showcase your talent. FOCUS is a celebration of the democratic nature of digital art and hence will be an open themed group photo show.

Co-ordinators

Arnim Rawat - +91-9919973667
arnim.rawat1998@gmail.com

B. Shubhangi - +91-7567957360
bshubhangi1998@gmail.com

Rules

1) Entries can be monochrome, black and white or color.
2) The images must solely be the work of entrant and the entrant must have the copyright to exhibit the same. APERTURE reserves the right to accept or refuse the work submitted. No individual assessment of images will be made available and the decision of selectors will be final.
3) Change of rules and/or time and acceptability of entries are at the discretion of the Organizing Committee member.
4) Copyright: If the image is selected by APERTURE, the entrant agrees and authorizes APERTURE to create copies, hang or use on walls/internet, books or publication or in any document and/or media created by APERTURE. In such usage, APERTURE will give due credit to the participant. Entrant also agrees, that APERTURE may use selected/ exhibited work to promote photography and/or the photographer at its discretion.
5) Entry fees: There is no fee of any kind to enter the exhibition. If your image is selected for the exhibition, APERTURE will cover all the cost of printing, framing etc. The entrant has the option of taking the printed and/or framed images after the exhibition after paying for the cost incurred in printing and framing.
6) Please do not mark your images with any kind of watermark (your name, copyright sign etc). Any image containing any watermark won’t be considered for the exhibition.
7) The image submitted shouldn’t be obscene or vulgar and shouldn’t contain any identifiable information about you or any third person.
8) You should have taken adequate permission and rights from the model(s) and the authority to submit the image for the exhibition.
9) Each participant can submit a maximum of 3 images.
10) High resolution copy of the photograph should be present with the photo.
11) There is no theme for the exhibition. In an overall, the exhibition should reflect the heterogeneity in creative expression by all the participants.

SHUTTER UP
On-the-spot Photography Contest

Shutter Up will test the promptness as well as the creativity of photographers. Not only the picture, the idea but also the thought process behind it is required to win this event. Participants will be given 3 hours’ time and a theme which will be disclosed on the spot.

Price Money

Prizes worth ₹2,500.

Co-ordinators

Tanush Bhagat - +91-9810047660

Rules

Participants are given limited time in which they can click and edit photos. NO FILTERS are allowed for the photographs. However, they can correct the basic settings of the photographs.
1. Only colored and/or RAW photos are to be submitted.
2. Participants cannot put any kind of water mark on their photos.
3. Minor adjustments like exposure and adding contrast is allowed.
4. If any of the above rule is violated, the APERTURE team will remove the photo from consideration.
5. All photos must be submitted in person. No proxy submission will be entertained.
6. All the photos must be submitted on or before the given deadline. The organisers have the right to refuse the entry which will be submitted late.
7. By agreeing to participate the participants agrees that in case of any dispute arising with regard to the photo credits or objection raised by subject captured in the photographs post submission, APERTURE will not hold responsibililty for the same.
8. Change of rules and/or time and acceptability of entries are at the discretion of the committee if required.
