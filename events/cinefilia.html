Rules

Team size: Solo

Judging Criteria: Content, Quality of humour, Stage presence, Audience engagement, Clarity of thought, Wackiness, USP(Unique Selling Point).

Eliminations

Eliminations are to be conducted on-ground during the fest, where the participants have to put on a show of upto 4 minutes in front of a live audience. After 3 minutes a single bell sound will be played. After 3 mins 30 secs double bell. At 4 mins the mic audio will be cut.

Finals

The participants who cleared the eliminations will be asked to prepare a sketch of maximum 5 minutes, to perform in front of a live audience. After 4 minutes a single bell sound will be played. After 4 mins 30 secs double bell. At 5 mins the mic audio will be cut.

Rules

General Rules

1.Mono-acting, puppetry, musicals are not allowed though ventriloquism is allowed. Use of props is allowed, brought in by participant himself.
2.The organisers hold the discretion of allowing it on stage.
3.Both English and Hindi are allowed. No other language is allowed.
4.Decisions taken by the judges is absolute and binding.
5.Use of obscenity/profanity(at the discretion of judges) is not allowed and there should be no direct implications.
6.Any Form of music is not allowed during the performance.
7.Since the participants are called upon in random order, each is required to be present at least 5 minutes prior to the start.
8.Organisers will not be responsible if you are unable to show up on time and miss your shot.
