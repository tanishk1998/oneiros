Nextar

Music of your choice, a dance floor majestic, a classic dance competition for all dance lovers to express, compete and win. Coming up with the rise and shine of new innovations, we are here again with NEXTAR that invites solo and duet dancers to contend in an event like never before. So bring about your best and be ready to be the peerless Next Star!

Prize Money

Solo: Winner - Rs. 8,000 & Runner’s Up - Rs. 5,000

Rules

1) The event is an on-stage event divided into two: Solo and Duet
2) Its a theme-free event and preference of track and dance form is completely up to you.
3) The event comprises of two rounds: preliminary and on-stage.
4) Freeze to the time limit of your performance:
For preliminary round (max. time limit)
SOLO: 1 min
DUET: 1 min 30 sec
For finals (max time limit)
SOLO: 3 min
DUET: 4 min
Participants will be marked negative for exceeding the time limits.
5) No extra time will be allotted for dressing up or rehearsals. Come dressed up accordingly for your performances.

Rules

6) Participants must get themselves registered in advance and report an hour prior to the event.
7) Registrations will close 2 hours prior to the event.
8) Participants must carry their college/institution IDs at all times.
9) Music track for the preliminary round and the finals may be different, but if same, the track would be stopped at 1 minute for solo and 1 min 30 sec for duet performances.
10) Participants are required to bring their tracks on at least 2 devices such as pen drives or phones for back up.
11) No form of vulgarity or indecency should be expressed in the performance.
12) The decision of the judges will be absolute.
13) The registration fee shall not be refunded for any reason.
14) No props are allowed
15) Use of powder, fire, liquids or any other substance that may dirty the venue is strictly prohibited.
16) The organizing committee has the right to cancel the event or a performance in any sort of unavoidable situations.
