Cluedo: A Murder Mystery

A murder story is created and clues are left all across the campus. Teams have to solve the murder mystery on the campus by tracing all the clues leading to the final crime scene. The team who solves the case first wins.

Venue

Room Number- 108 (1-AB).

Timing

10:00am - 12:30pm

Co-ordinators

Ananya Yadav

Garima Patel

Sanket Maheshwari

Prize Money

Prize: worth ₹1,500.

Rules

1) Maximum of four members per team.
2) The mystery hunt consists of some clues, each one leading to next clue. Each correctly solved clue
carries 10 points. Solving the final mystery carries 100 points.
3) Clues are to be found in a particular order. A team cannot skip a clue.
4) Skipping of a clue leads to disqualification of the team.
5) Do not search for clues in off-limit areas.
6) If you found other team's clue by any chance, never tamper it. A violation of this rule will cause disqualification of the team.
7) You move any object to search the clue, kindly replace it back.
8) All the clues of the team are different, but the final destination i.e. the crime scene is same.
9) The team which finds the murderer first, wins.
10) Registration fees ₹200/- per team.

Game of Choices: You win or you Lose

3 rounds to test your knowledge in 3 different domains.
•Marvel and DC
•Game of Thrones or Harry Potter
•Memes

Co-ordinators

Aastha Vyas

Agrani Swankar

Prize Money

Prizes : worth ₹1,500.

Rules

-> Maximum of 4 members per team are allowed.
-> For round 1:
1. Every team will have to choose between- Marvel or DC.
2. Each team will be asked 4 questions of 10 marks each.
3. They will be given 45 seconds for each question.
4. At the end of 3 questions, the participating teams will be given an opportunity to ask one bonus

1. Every team will have to choose between - Game of Thrones or Harry Potter.

3. One member of the team would have to draw the clue given and all the other players would have to guess.
4. They will be given 60 seconds for each question.
-> For round 3:
1. There will be 10 questions in this round. Team pressing the buzzer first will be given the chance to answer.
2. 15 marks will be awarded for each correct answer & 5 marks will be deducted for each incorrect answer.
3. Team will be given 45 seconds for each question.

Human Ludo: Into the Fantasy World

Human Ludo: Into the Fantasy World

Two teams of two students each should be sent to the arena to play the game. The team members will wear the same color t-shirts. They have to move forward and go to the finish line according to the role of the single die. Each player rolls the die. Players alternate turns in the clockwise direction.

Venue

Right Side of Amul cafeteria *(AB―1)

Timing

10:00am Onwards.

Co-ordinators

Amit Yash Mishra

Rohan Choudhary

Rules

Each team rolls the die; the highest roller begins the game. Team alternate turns in a clockwise
direction. To enter a token into play from its yard to its starting square, a team must roll a 6. If the
team has no tokens yet in play and rolls other than a 6, the turn passes to the next team.
Registration Fees: 50 per Person.
