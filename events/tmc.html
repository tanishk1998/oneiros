SONG SMITH

Song Smith

The classic draw and guess game, but with a slight twist. In teams of two, with three rounds each including the emoji-nary,the taboo round and the classic Pictionary round, one player draws the words given on the chit (movie,show,object,character), and the other player guesses. In the case of emoji-nary, words on the chit but with emojis. Verbal communication between the two players is not allowed. Hosting Clubs: TMC & Litmus

Co-ordinators

Anurag Singh (LITMUS) : +919711895467
iamscas@gmail.com

Aakanksha Singh (TMC) : +918860575470
aakankshasingh236@gmail.com

Rules

A team of 2-4 people.
1· Topic to be chosen by chit-picking.
2· Can write in both Hindi, and English.
3· One member of a team to write for two minutes, and then to pass on the lyric to the next
member, and so on, in sync with the tune. This happens for two rounds, hence, every member
of the team writes twice, for a cumulative time of four minutes.
4· Members will not be allowed to talk when the lyrics are being written down.
5· An added time of 10 minutes for discussion of the composition.
6· Performance cannot exceed 2 minutes.
7· Every member of the team to perform on the stage.
8· Backing tracks and accompanist DO NOT carry extra marks, while being judged.
9· Backing track/instrument will not be provided to any participant.

Judging basis:
· Lyrical Sense – 40%
· Vocal Inputs – 40%
· Team Dynamics – 10%
· Stage Presence – 10%
