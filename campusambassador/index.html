Be Our Representative

Work of a Campus Ambassador

As a Campus Ambassador, you are expected to act as a link between the fest and your college. As a result, you are expected to:

  • Publicize – Promoting Oneiros’18 posts on various social media platforms like Facebook, Snapchat, Instagram. Ensure online publicity of Oneiros’18 through social media, in your city and especially in your college.
  • Encourage and facilitate active participation in the fest from your college. Communicate – You will be responsible for managing Oneiros, Manipal University Jaipur’s presence in your college.
  • Lead – Select CA’s will be given the opportunity to lead their college contingent during Oneiros.

Incentives

Incentives

  • Prizes such as headphones, mobiles and a lot more for the top performing Campus Ambassadors on the basis of performance.
  • Bi-weekly and monthly incentives plus prizes through the CA of month program.
  • The coveted Campus Ambassador certificate authenticating their work for Oneiros’18, Manipal University.
  • Letters of recommendation for exceptional performance.
  • Free accommodation on campus for the entire duration of Oneiros’18 for top performing CA’s.
  • Discount coupons on partner products and stalls at Oneiros’18.

Opportunities

Learning Opportunities

We at Oneiros’18, Manipal University Jaipur consider our campus ambassadors to be an integral part of the team.

  • A virtue tour of Oneiros’18 allowing you to interact with people managing the various departments of Oneiros’18 and get a first-hand idea of how different facets of one of the largest cultural fest in are managed.
  • Club workshops: We at Manipal University Jaipur have 10 clubs which conduct events. Some of them include music, dance, literary, quizzing, etc. which has forged a name for them in the college circuit. We plan to conduct introductory workshops involving these clubs, where the participants will be given a flavor of that particular field.
  • Co-ordination and public relations – Working to build associations will help you learn how to manage public relations and also create a giant network.
